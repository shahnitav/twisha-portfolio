Working Drawing

Kitchen

Year: 2018 (Sem V)
Brief: The aim of the studio was to understand and study the brand, and the given site and its constraints. And implying this to design a retail store for the brand.
Site: Golden Icon Centre, Vadodara, Gujarat, India.

The apple devices, apart from providing the most convenient look, provide users with the easiest OS. When focused on its design principles, Apple makes use of the Golden Ratio in their products, that can be noted through the study of its logo and its devices. The brand focuses on transparency, through customer services, and the work environment in the Apple corporates. These two factors have been taken forward in the design to give the users an experience that is convenient and reflects the company’s vision. The store has been designed for the customers to walk in the golden ratio, moving from larger spaces to smaller ones, giving a new experience to the users justifying Apple’s philosophy.
